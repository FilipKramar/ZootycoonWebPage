About Zoo Tycoon 2

Welcome to the world of Zoo Tycoon 2! It's an exciting and immersive simulation game that allows you to create and manage your very own virtual zoo. With a wide range of animals, customizable exhibits, and thrilling challenges, Zoo Tycoon 2 offers endless possibilities for animal lovers and simulation enthusiasts.

In Zoo Tycoon 2, you'll step into the shoes of a zoo manager, responsible for every aspect of your zoo's success. From designing and constructing habitats to choosing the perfect selection of animals, it's up to you to create a captivating environment that both animals and visitors will love.
